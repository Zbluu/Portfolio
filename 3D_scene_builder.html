Aside


A standalone 3D scene builder software used to construct & reproduct a building or a place.

The goal was to make a software to build and share 3D scenes. You can easily recreate your own building company, your town or a room.

This software was made with Unreal Engine 4 in C++ completed by Blueprints.
It is connected to a MongoDB database to store all the 3D scenes and allow to share it with other users.
