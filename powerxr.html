Aside


An AR mobile game where you shoot a projectile in real life to play!

This is an augmented reality game when you choose a projectile in the real life, make some calibration in the app to detect easily your projectile (that is filmed by your phone during the game), then you choose a target in the real life, and shoot your projectile to it. The result of your shoot will be reproduced in the game!


Made with Unreal Engine 4 in C++ completed by Blueprints. I used OpenCV to do image processing to detect projectiles. Compatible with Android & PC.
