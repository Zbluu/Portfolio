Aside


A mobile game where you need to use the gyroscope to shoot & defend your village.

In this game, you need to teleport yourself on the map to shoot the army of legionnaires before they destroy your village.
Play strategically, choose a spot to shoot, teleport to it then aim and shoot with the gyroscope of your phone.



This game has been made in Unreal Engine 4, mostly with Blueprints. It is compatible with Android and iOS.

An interesting thing here is that all the programing work had to be the most flexible and configurable possible, to allow to change 3D models/locations, UIs, game difficulty and everything else without my help. My client wanted to be able to make the game evolve, even after my job was done.
