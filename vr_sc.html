Aside


VR application (HTC) to showcase high-tech & smart office products.

In partnership with SharingCloud and Lacave Paris, we created a VR application used in a public event to showcase some smart office products in a virtual simulation that takes place in the buildings of a company.

You can find a scenario example just below:


The application has been made in Unreal Engine 4, mostly with Blueprints. We used an HTC Vive Pro for the VR version, but it is playable on a simple PC with mouse and keyboard.

My job here was to do all of the programing work: create a scenario, interact with 3D objects, move the player, manage collisions, show UIs and indications, make sure that it is VR compatible and test it.
